#  *Peaky Blinders*

## **Overview**
**Peaky Blinders** is a British crime drama television series created by *Steven Knight*.  
It follows the powerful **Shelby crime family** in **Birmingham, England**, after World War I.  
The show is known for its **stylish cinematography**, **intense storytelling**, and **strong characters**.

---

### **Main Characters**
- **Thomas Shelby (Cillian Murphy)** – The ambitious and intelligent leader of the Peaky Blinders  
- **Arthur Shelby (Paul Anderson)** – Thomas’s hot-headed older brother  
- **Polly Gray (Helen McCrory)** – The strong and wise aunt who helps run the family business  
- **Ada Shelby (Sophie Rundle)** – The only Shelby sister, often caught between family and freedom  
- **Alfie Solomons (Tom Hardy)** – A clever and unpredictable gangster ally  

---

### **Famous Quote**
> “You can change what you do, but you can’t change what you want.”  
> — *Thomas Shelby*

---

### **Why I Love It**
1.  The **1920s setting** and detailed costumes are visually stunning.  
2.  **Cillian Murphy’s performance** as Thomas Shelby is powerful and emotional.  
3.  The **modern rock soundtrack** adds a unique twist to a historical drama.  
4.  The story mixes **family loyalty, ambition, and power struggles** perfectly.  

---

### **Fun Fact**
The Peaky Blinders were **based on a real gang** that operated in Birmingham during the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
